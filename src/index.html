What We Do

Platform-agnostic, non-fiction storytelling.

Dagger Stories makes documentaries, reports from the field and manages productions. In addition to developing and running projects, we provide loan-outs for directing, producing, research and development services.

Let’s cut through the shit and build new narratives together.

About

Hi, I’m Dagna Gallinger. I’m a director, producer, and the head of Dagger Stories.

I’ve spent most of my career working behind the scenes, for big machines like VICE, CBC and CTV. I made sure current affairs shows ran like clockwork, producing moments that make you cry, laugh and think.

I also take ideas and run with them. Over the years I’ve helped create shows and one-offs. My work has been broadcast in over 15 countries and has taken me to Mexico, Honduras, Russia, Israel, the Palestinian Territories, the United States, Canada, and across Europe.

Now I develop my own projects from scratch in collaboration with subject insiders and high-calibre creatives. I am looking for original stories, but it’s not just about novelty. In order to do a story justice, and to capture the interest of our audiences, we must innovate and find new narratives, new lenses through which we see our protagonists and subject matter.
