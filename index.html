About

Hi, I’m Dagna Gallinger. I’m a director and producer.

I’ve spent most of my career working behind the scenes, for places like VICE, CBC and CTV. I made sure current-affairs shows ran like clockwork, producing moments that make you laugh, cry and think.

I also take ideas and run with them. Over the years I’ve helped create shows and one-offs. My work has been broadcast in over 15 countries and has taken me to Mexico, Honduras, Russia, Israel, the Palestinian Territories, the United States, Canada and across Europe.

Now I develop my own projects from scratch in collaboration with subject insiders and high-calibre creatives. I am looking for original stories, to innovate and to find new narratives.

My work has been featured on outlets including Paramount Plus, Amazon Prime, Hulu, Apple TV, Channel 4, National Geographic Latinoamérica, Canal+, CBC Gem, Investigation Discovery, SBS On Demand and many more.
